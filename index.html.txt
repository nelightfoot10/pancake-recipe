    <p> This is a common breakfast item used to make a quick meal. Great to cook for children or large gatherings.</p>

    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>1 eggs</li>
      <li>1 cups of flour</li>
      <li>1 1/2 cups water</li>
      <li>1/2 cups of baking powder</li>
      <li>3 tbsp of sugar</li>   
    </ul>
    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>In a bowl add flour, eggs,baking powder and sugar</li>
      <li>Use whisk and mix until the mix is not thick or watery</li>
      <li>In a saucepan melt or heat oil in the pan evenly</li> 
      <li>Once pan is coated and hot, pour even amounts of pancake mix on to the pan, flip when you see the uncooked side bubble for a nice crispy edge</li>
    </ol>
